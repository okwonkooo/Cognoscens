Cognoscens

A tool to help people learn languages by being presented with random images and attempting to describe them in their target language. It was made specifically for IB students who are studying a language at an ab initio level for their IAs.

How to use?

Step 1

Press

Step 2

As soon as you're able to see the image, a timer will start. Either a) start describing the image out loud or b) write it down on the text-box directly below the image.

Step 3

Once the timer has ended or you feel like there's nothing else to say, move on to the next image.

Step 4

Repeat step 2 and 3 for the other images.

Step 5

Repeat as many times as you want.
